General Questions

Are there any safety requirements?
Everyone needs to wear a helmet while skateboarding, rock climbing, batting, playing baseball and ziplining.
Are there age limits?
Children must be a minimum of 7 years old.
Do we need to fill out a waiver?
Everyone must complete their own individual waiver. Anyone under the age of 18 must have their waiver forms signed by a parent or legal guardian.
