Come and Explore Skatedevils Urban Jungle!

We are a one of a kind activity park open from 8am to 10pm, every day of the week, and we feature not one but three skateboarding rinks! Bring your family and friends to try out our rock climbing walls, our batting cages, and our baseball and soccer fields! But wait there's more!! Fly through the air on our top of the line zip line and head on over to our snack bar and pro shop for a variety of equipment and merchandise.
